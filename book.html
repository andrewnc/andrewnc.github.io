Everyday Data Science

Life is full of decisions. We, as people, have the remarkable ability to make decisions in the face of uncertainty. We have recently developed the ability to use computers to process vast amount of data to improve our decision making. This book is written to give you tools and inspire aspiring decision makers. You make decisions daily and the methods from data science can help.

Everyday Data Science

Everyday data science is a collection tools and techniques you can master to use data science in your day-to-day life. Some examples include, A/B testing for lemonade, word vectors to improve your resume, differential equations for weight loss, and how a man used statistics to qualify for the Olympics.
