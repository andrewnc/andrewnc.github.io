Welcome to the Blog

I have wanted to start a blog for a year or more. Before I started, I wanted to make sure the topic was consistent, and I something I could write about for a long while. So here we are, data science for esoteric programming languages. Not only will this help my data science skills stay sharp, but it will be an awesome way for me to justify my eso-lang hobby.

Well, the first order of business is to clarify my definition of esoteric programming language.

Axiomatically, if you post on the internet people will disagree with you. So, it follows that people will disagree with my choice of languages. That's ok. Some will be more mainstream than others, but I hope that none are in the top 50 or so on Stack Overflow or Github.

Although, I actually plan on doing an OCaml series. Which is definitely not esoteric, but ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ it'll still be fun, and educational.

Over the next several months I'll release a series of blog posts using J. I'll update this welcome post with top viewed articles. And with that, let's get started
