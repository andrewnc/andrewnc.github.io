Pong

When asked if I knew anything about Aritificial intellegence, I want to be able to say yes! One of the best ways to learn is by doing, and trying. So I wrote a game of pong - and made some bots that you could test your skills against. There are 3 levels of bots each of them use different calculations to try and hit the ball:

  1. Derp AI
  2. Smart AI
  3. Impossible to beat AI
It takes some configuration to get it up and running, since I didn't build it to distribute, but it is was a very fun project.
